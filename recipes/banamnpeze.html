Fried Plantains

Description

Often considered the national dish of Haiti, Plantains are a staple in pretty much every haitian meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Canola Oil
  • 2 Large Green Plantains, peeled, sliced 1 1/2" (6-8 slices per plantain)
  • Bowl of Hot Water
  • Salt

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a frying pan. Brown pieces on all sides over medium-high heat til golden brown about 4 minutes.
  2. Using a plantain press/tostonera, brown paper bag or a cutting board & a pot or a plate flatten the fried plantains.
  3. In a prep bowl filled with hot water & 2 tablespoons of salt soak the flattened fried plantains into the salt water for 1 minute.
  4. Shake off excess water, gently place back into hot oil & refry the plantains again. Drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  5. Salt to taste.
  6. Serve hot.
